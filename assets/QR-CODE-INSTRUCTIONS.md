# Google Pay QR Code Instructions

## How to Generate Your Google Pay QR Code

### Option 1: Using Google Pay App
1. Open Google Pay app on your phone
2. Tap on your profile picture (top right)
3. Select "QR code"
4. Tap "Share" or take a screenshot
5. Save the QR code image

### Option 2: Using UPI QR Code Generator
1. Visit any UPI QR code generator website
2. Enter your UPI ID: `compunetcenter@okaxis`
3. Enter business name: `CompuNet Computer Center`
4. Generate and download the QR code

### Option 3: Request from Your Bank
1. Contact your bank (Axis Bank)
2. Request a UPI QR code for your business account
3. They will provide a printable/digital QR code

## Adding the QR Code to Your Website

1. Save your QR code image as `qr-code.png` or `qr-code.jpg`
2. Place it in the `/public/assets/` directory
3. The website will automatically display it on the payment page

## QR Code Specifications

- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Size**: 500x500px recommended
- **Quality**: High resolution for clear scanning
- **Background**: White or transparent
