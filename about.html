Our History

CompuNet Computer Center has been a trusted name in computer education in Rajasthan, dedicated to providing quality training to students from all backgrounds. Our institute has successfully trained thousands of students, helping them achieve their career goals through comprehensive computer education programs.

We started with a vision to make computer education accessible and affordable for everyone. Over the years, we have expanded our course offerings to include government-certified programs, professional software training, and skill development courses that meet industry standards.
